About us

Shebi Enterprises is a Civil Contractor firm Established in 2005 founded by Jerald Francis. At Shebi Enterprises, we are focused on providing services with the highest levels of customer satisfaction, we do everything we can to meet your expectations. We are one of the most respected cladding works Contractors in Mumbai.

What we do ?

With a variety of offerings to choose from, we’re sure you’ll be happy working with us. We are specialists in Stone dry cladding , Wet cladding , A.C.P cladding , Glazing Work , M.S Fabrication Work, Structural glass, Sliding Glass , All Interior and Exterior cladding (stone and glass) work.
